Career

Currently, I run my own independent design and Web3 practice, partnering with collectors, founders, and communities across NFT, art, and creative tech. Most of my work focuses on turning raw ideas into finished products — identity, artwork, site, and contract, all shipped by one person.

Over the past few years, I've launched collections like Punkers V2, Manthy, and the original Punkers, built products like Underdrip, YourSpace, and Odds Launchpad, and run live art events like SipandDrip and Mural24Jam.

Today, I bring all of that together through visual design, NFT art direction, and AI-assisted coding — mostly for Web3 communities and creative products.
